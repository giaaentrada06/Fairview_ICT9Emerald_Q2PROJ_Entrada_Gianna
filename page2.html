Breakfast!
Tapsilog

Ingredients:

  • 1 lb. beef sirloin sliced thinly
  • 3 pieces eggs
  • 6 tablespoons cooking oil
  • 3 tablespoons Knorr Liquid Seasoning
  • 6 cloves crushed garlic
  • ¾ cups pineapple juice
  • 2 tablespoons brown sugar
  • ¼ teaspoon ground white pepper
  • 5 cups leftover rice
  • 1 teaspoon salt
Instructions:
  • Prepare the tapa by marinating beef in the marinade ingredients. Refrigerate overnight.
  • For garlic fried rice (sinangag), heat oil in a skillet, cook garlic until golden brown, then stir-fry rice with salt for 3–5 minutes.
  • Cook tapa by boiling with water and reducing the liquid, then frying the beef in oil until brown.
  • Fry eggs in oil until the yolk is fully cooked through steam.

Lunch!
Sisig

Ingredients:

  • 1 lb pig ears
  • 1 lb pig snout
  • 1 lb pork belly
  • 2 pieces onions, minced
  • 3 pieces bay leaves
  • 2 teaspoons salt
  • 4 thumbs ginger, crushed
  • 1 quart water

Instructions:

  • Boil pig ears, snout, and pork belly with water, salt, ginger, and bay leaves until tender.
  • Grill the boiled meats until crispy. Chop into small pieces.
  • Mix the chopped meat with onions, calamansi juice, and optional chili. Serve on a sizzling plate.

Dinner!
Tinola

Ingredients:

  • 1 tablespoon canola oil
  • 1 small onion, peeled and sliced thinly
  • 3 cloves garlic, peeled and minced
  • 2 thumb-sized fresh ginger
  • 1 whole chicken (3–4 pounds), cut into serving pieces
  • 2 tablespoons fish sauce
  • 5 cups water
  • 1 small green papaya, pared, seeded, and cut into 2-inch wedges
  • 1 bunch fresh spinach leaves
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions:

  • In a pot over medium heat, heat oil. Add onions, ginger, and garlic. Cook until softened.
  • Add chicken and cook until lightly browned.
  • Add fish sauce and water. Simmer for 30 minutes.
  • Add papaya and cook until tender. Season with salt and pepper.
  • Add spinach and cook until wilted. Serve hot.
